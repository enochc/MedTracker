Privacy Policy for MedTracker

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Privacy Summary: MedTracker does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All medication information stays on your device.

1. Information Collection and Use

MedTracker is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you enter into MedTracker, including medication names, dosages, notes, and logging history, is stored exclusively on your device using local SQLite database storage.

2. Data Storage

All medication data is stored locally on your Android device in an encrypted application sandbox. This data includes:

  • Medication names and dosages
  • Timestamps of when medications were taken
  • Optional notes and amounts
  • Widget configuration preferences

This data is never transmitted over the internet and remains under your exclusive control.

3. No Account Required

MedTracker does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or supply any personally identifiable information to use the app.

4. No Analytics or Tracking

MedTracker does not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or third-party data collection tools. We do not track your app usage, medication patterns, or any other behavioral data.

5. No Advertisements

MedTracker does not display advertisements and does not integrate with any advertising networks or platforms.

6. Permissions

MedTracker requests only the permissions necessary for core functionality:

  • Widget Permissions: To place and update home screen widgets
  • Storage Permissions: To maintain local database of your medications

No network permissions are requested or used by the app.

7. Data Security

While your data stays on your device, we recommend:

  • Using your device's built-in security features (screen lock, encryption)
  • Regularly backing up your device if you want to preserve medication history
  • Being cautious about who has physical access to your device

8. Data Deletion

You maintain complete control over your data. To delete all MedTracker data:

  • Uninstall the app from your device, or
  • Clear app data through Android Settings → Apps → MedTracker → Storage → Clear Data

9. Children's Privacy

MedTracker does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app can be used by individuals of any age under appropriate supervision, but no data is collected regardless of the user's age.

10. Third-Party Services

MedTracker does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or external platforms. The app operates entirely offline.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Open Source

MedTracker is built using open-source technologies. The app's code can be reviewed to verify these privacy claims if you wish to audit the app's behavior.

14. Your Rights

Since MedTracker does not collect any data about you, there is no data to access, correct, or delete from our servers. You have complete control over the data stored locally on your device.
